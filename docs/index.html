Welcome to Yogabot, your beginner yoga trainer.

This is an application that requires two people. We will go through three poses: the plank, the hand-to-foot, and the tree pose. Perform one of the poses and start excersising. After you click start a 5-second countdown will begin, and then Yogabot will attempt to detect the pose. If the pose is recognized correctly, your score will increase and be shown on the screen.
